Kiln of the First Flame

Once you have defeated the 4 Lords of Cinder and placed their ashes on their empty thrones, speak to the Fire Keeper, then activate the Bonfire, which will lead to a cutscene. Afterwards, you will be transported to the first Bonfire at the Kiln of the First Flame, which looks like a dark copy of Firelink Shrine. Take the path outside and activate the bonfire to proceed to the First Flame. Upon arriving, light the Bonfire to the right, head up the path and enter the clearing to battle the Soul of Cinder. Defeat him to earn Soul of the Lords. Be wary that it is possible to invade at the path, before the fog gate. Activating the Bonfire in front warps to Dreg Keep instead.

Kiln of the First Flame is a Location in Dark Souls 3. In order to reach this area, the player must first kill all Lords of Cinder and speak to the Fire Keeper in Firelink Shrine. Interacting with the Firelink bonfire will then warp the player to the first bonfire. See the Dark Souls 3 Walkthrough or Game Progress Route for a path to the game's Endings.
