Irithyll of the Boreal Valley

Irithyll is a city found in the Boreal Valley, characterized by steepled buildings and partially covered by snow. A great cathedral lies at its center, dedicated to the Darkmoon. An eternal moon hangs above the city, never fading.

Irithyll is the city that was created below the ruins of ancient Anor Londo, of which only the central cathedral remains intact. It became the home of the noble descendants of the Gods who worshiped the moon and Gwyndolin, the new king of the gods
