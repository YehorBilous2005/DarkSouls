Smouldering Lake

This was once the home to the daughters of the Witch of Izalith and the demons born from the Flame of Chaos. Unlike the First Flame, which is tied to the souls of the undead, the faded Flame of Chaos was tied to the souls of the demons, and so the lifeless bodies of Capra, Taurus, Asylum and a single Centipede Demon can be found strewn throughout the ruins and piled high near the Old Demon King, one of the last remaining demons of old. A petrified arachnoid body, resembling Quelaag's Sister, lies within the ruins, where a humanoid corpse holds the Quelana Pyromancy Tome, indicated that the sisters lived here at one point.

The Smouldering Lake is an optional location in Dark Souls III. It is a subsection of the Catacombs of Carthus.
